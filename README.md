# ldjam59
## Lore
Borjomi the Bat got hit in the head by a wind turbine (thanks, Obama) and now his echo-location only works when he flaps his wings!
Help him navigate his way trough life with this unfortunate condition.

## How to play
SPACEBAR or CLICK or TAP SCREEN to flap Borjomi's wings, you know how to play this game... but perhaps not with faulty sonar!
